About S.L.V Plywoods & Hardware


Founded in 1995 by Kanumuri Srinivasulu Naidu, SLV Plywood and Hardware has been a trusted name in the plywood and hardware industry for over 30 years. Built on the pillars of trust, honesty, and dedication , our business has grown from a humble shop into a leading wholesale supplier in the region. From the very beginning. Mrs. Kanumuri Sulochana joined the journey, bringing her own passion and attention to detail. Together, they have created a family-run enterprise known for its reliability, integrity.
At SLV Plywood and Hardware. Our loyal customers and partners are a testament to our unwavering focus on quality and service. For three decades, we have proudly supplied plywood, doors, hardware, and interior solutions to countless homes and projects, always standing by our core values. We look forward to continuing this legacy, serving our community with the same trust and honesty that built our business from the start.
